I am a second year PhD student at Electrical and Computer Engineering department at the Rice University under supervision of Prof. Richard Baraniuk. Before that, I got my B.Sc in Electrical Engineering from Sharif University of Technology. During my BSc studies, I have been researching in statistical signal and image processing, machine learning, sparse signal recovery, inverse problems, and computational biology in Digital Signal Processing Laboratory and Advanced Communications Research Institute at the Sharif University of Technology. I spent the 2018 summer as a research assistant in the Laboratory for Bioinformatics and Computational Biology at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.
