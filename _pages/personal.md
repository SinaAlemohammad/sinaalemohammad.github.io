I was born in Tehran, Iran in 11th August 1995. I have one little sister, Sara, and one little brother, Soroush. I spent my primary and secondary education in [Nikan School](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nikan_High_School) before comming to [Sharif University of Technology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sharif_University_of_Technology).

Here is the list of my hobbies

- Soccer

I've enjoyed being a goalkeeper since early days and I was the goalkeeper of my school team. In the last year of high school, my Anterior Cruciate Ligament (ACL) torn while I was playing soccer. It took me 4 years to fully recover from that injury and then I became the goalkeeper of the Sharif University of Technology Electrical Engineering futsal team, where we got the championship in Sharif University Futsal Tournament this semester.
